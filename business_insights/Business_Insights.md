RECOMMENDATIONS

1. Recommendation 1: Expand the Clothing Product Line

Since Clothing contributes over 50% of total revenue, management should:

-Increase inventory levelS
-Expand product variety

Expected Result: Higher average order value and increased revenue.

2. Recommendation 2: Grow Subscription Memberships

Subscribers spend 16% more than non-subscribers.

Actions:

Offer exclusive discounts
Create loyalty rewards
Provide early access to new products
Introduce referral incentives

Expected Result: Increased customer lifetime value.

3. Recommendation 3: Focus Marketing on YouTH

The 18–25 segment represents the largest customer group.

Actions:

Increase social media advertising
Partner with influencers
Use student-focused promotions

Expected Result: Improved customer acquisition and retention.

4. Recommendation 4: Prepare Aggressively for Winter

Winter is the highest-performing season.

Actions:

Increase inventory before winter
Launch winter sales campaigns early
Promote outerwear bundles
Offer seasonal discounts

Expected Result: Maximize revenue during peak demand periods.

5. Recommendation 5: Target Female Shoppers

Female customers generate nearly 60% of revenue.

Actions:

Personalize marketing campaigns
Expand product ranges aligned with purchasing trends
Create loyalty programs specifically for high-value female customers

Expected Result: Increased repeat purchases and customer loyalty.

6. Recommendation 6: Create Premium Customer Segments

Identify customers whose purchase amounts exceed 75th percentile and offer:

VIP memberships
Exclusive offers
Personalized recommendations
Premium customer support

Expected Result: Increased retention of high-value customers.
